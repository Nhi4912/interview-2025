## 🧠 Growth Mindset / Tư Duy Phát Triển

### When You Feel Stuck / Khi Bạn Cảm Thấy Bế Tắc

1. **It's normal** — Everyone gets stuck. Even senior engineers at FAANG struggled at first.
2. **Review the pattern** — Read Pattern Trigger section. Most problems are variations.
3. **Take a break** — 15 min walk, come back fresh. The brain solves problems in background.
4. **Lower the bar** — Can't solve Medium? Go back to related Easy. Build up.
5. **Talk it out** — Explain the problem out loud (rubber duck debugging works for LeetCode too).

### When You Want to Quit / Khi Bạn Muốn Bỏ Cuộc

1. **Remember WHY** — Better job, better salary, better life for family.
2. **Look at progress** — Scroll up. Look at those ✅ milestones. You've come far.
3. **Just do ONE** — Don't think about 200. Just solve 1 today. That's all.
4. **Compound effect** — 1 problem/day × 200 days = interview ready. It adds up.
5. **You're not behind** — Everyone has their own timeline. Consistency > speed.

### Interview Day Mindset / Tư Duy Ngày Phỏng Vấn

1. **You've prepared** — Months of SRS means patterns are in long-term memory.
2. **Communicate** — Talk through UMPIRE steps out loud. Interviewers want to hear your thinking.
3. **It's a conversation** — Not a test. They want to work with you.
4. **Stuck is OK** — Say "Let me think about this differently." Ask clarifying questions.
5. **One round doesn't define you** — Even if one round goes poorly, others can save you.
